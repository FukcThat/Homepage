My mess

Memory Card

A memory card game with a twist. Avoid clicking the same card twice within a round. Three difficulty modes you can level up thorugh.

Shopping Cart

A pretend online shop where you can put products in a cart, see the total price, check out items in detail and check out your cart.

Tic-Tac-Toe

A simple tic-tac-toe game. Choose your sign, enter your names and play as many rounds as you like.

Library

A site to keep track of your own little library. Add the books you have, update the read status and watch your collection grow.

Calculator

A simple calculator. This is basically useless since you likely already have a better build in one but I made it pretty and it works fine for simple math.

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors. Everyone knows the rules. Not much to it but I made the cursor reflect your choice so it looks very cute.
